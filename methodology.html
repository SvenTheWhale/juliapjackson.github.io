Continuity Methodology

BIA — Identify Critical Capabilities

  • Engage leaders and SMEs to define scope and relevant capabilities.
  • Tier capabilities (1–5) and focus on Tier 1–3 for deeper analysis.
  • Identify additional SMEs for dependency discovery.

BIA — Map Dependencies

  • Group capabilities logically (EHS, FSQR, Ops, Receiving, Shipping).
  • Identify applications (with RTOs), technologies, equipment, third parties, and roles.
  • Evaluate criticality and operational impact.

Continuity Strategies & Runbooks

  • Facilitate “what if” sessions with SMEs to define practical continuity steps.
  • Document strategies in clear, standardized formats.
  • Iterate with SMEs and leadership for clarity and approval.
  • Promote final strategies to production and communicate availability.
