Methodology

A Structured, Standards‑Aligned Approach

My methodology blends business systems analysis, continuity governance, and structured problem‑solving. I use a consistent who/what/when/why/how approach to uncover root causes, clarify expectations, and ensure that documentation and decisions reflect real operational flow. This structure supports both business continuity work and broader operational analysis.

I align work to ISO 22301, ISO 22318, NIST guidance, and corporate governance requirements, translating standards into clear, practical expectations that teams can understand and use. I focus on creating documentation that is accurate, repeatable, and grounded in how the business actually operates.

Core Components of My Methodology

1. Understanding the Operational Reality

I begin by mapping how work actually moves through a process or system. This includes identifying critical steps, dependencies, data flows, and decision points. I partner with SMEs to validate assumptions and ensure that documentation reflects real practice, not inherited or outdated expectations.

2. Clarifying Requirements and Constraints

I translate business needs, regulatory requirements, and governance expectations into clear, testable requirements. This includes identifying constraints, surfacing risks, and ensuring that upstream and downstream impacts are understood before work begins.

3. Structured Documentation and Standards Alignment

I create documentation that is consistent, traceable, and aligned with standards. This includes:

  • Business process documentation
  • Continuity strategies and recovery expectations
  • Data mapping and system behavior analysis
  • Governance requirements and audit‑ready artifacts

I focus on clarity, accuracy, and repeatability so teams can rely on documentation during both day‑to‑day operations and high‑pressure events.

4. Cross‑Functional Facilitation

I bring together functional teams, IT, compliance, and operations to ensure shared understanding. I facilitate working sessions that surface gaps, dependencies, and risks early, reducing rework and strengthening decision‑making.

5. Agile Delivery and Iterative Improvement

I manage Agile delivery within my workstreams, including backlog refinement, sprint planning, and coordination with product owners and technical leads. I support Scrum ceremonies and maintain clear documentation so teams stay aligned and unblocked. I treat documentation and processes as living assets that evolve with the business.

6. Risk‑Aware Decision Support

I help teams understand the operational, regulatory, and customer impacts of decisions. I identify where established practices no longer meet expectations and offer practical, flexible improvements that enhance clarity, resilience, and audit credibility without disrupting what already works.

How This Methodology Shows Up in My Work

  • The Runbook framework at Cargill
  • Customer audit response models
  • Regulatory and cross‑border requirements at Securian
  • Data mapping and migration analysis at Green Tree / Ditech

Across all environments, the methodology remains consistent: understand the work, clarify expectations, align to standards, document clearly, and support teams in making informed, risk‑aware decisions.
